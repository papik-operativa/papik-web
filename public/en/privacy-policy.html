2 · Purposes of processing

PAPIK Group processes users' personal data for the following purposes:

``` A · To respond to requests for information, quotes or contact received through the website forms or by email.

B · To manage the contractual and pre-contractual relationship with clients who request construction, development, retrofit or other PAPIK Group services.

C · To send commercial communications, newsletters or information about PAPIK Group services, always with the user's prior express consent.

D · To comply with the legal, tax and accounting obligations applicable to the business.

E · To carry out aggregated statistical analysis of the website for service improvement, without identifying the individual user. ```

3 · Legal basis

The legal basis for processing depends on the specific purpose:

``` · Purpose A (handling requests): pre-contractual measures at the data subject's request (article 6.1.b GDPR) or the data subject's consent (article 6.1.a GDPR).

· Purpose B (contractual relationship): performance of a contract (article 6.1.b GDPR).

· Purpose C (commercial communications): the data subject's express consent (article 6.1.a GDPR), revocable at any time.

· Purpose D (legal obligations): compliance with legal obligations (article 6.1.c GDPR).

· Purpose E (aggregated statistics): the controller's legitimate interest (article 6.1.f GDPR), after balancing it against the data subject's rights. ```

4 · Retention period

Personal data is kept only for as long as is strictly necessary for the purpose for which it was collected:

``` · Information requests not converted into a contractual relationship: for the reasonable time needed to handle the request and, afterwards, up to 12 months unless the user expressly consents to a longer period.

· Contractual relationships: for the duration of the relationship and, afterwards, for the periods legally required to comply with tax obligations (minimum 4 years), accounting obligations (6 years) and the ten-year guarantee applicable to construction works (10 years), as required under Spanish law.

· Commercial communications: until the user withdraws consent.

· Aggregated statistics: until service termination or objection request. ```

· Financial institutions in the case of mortgage management requiring their intermediation, always with the data subject's express consent.

The user can exercise at any time the rights granted by data protection law:

`` · Right of access to their personal data. · Right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data. · Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"). · Right to object to processing. · Right to restriction of processing. · Right to data portability. · Right not to be subject to automated individual decisions. · Right to withdraw consent at any time, without retroactive effect. ``

8 · Complaints to the Spanish Data Protection Agency

Users have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) if they consider that the processing of their personal data does not comply with applicable law.

The AEPD's contact details are available at https://www.aepd.es.

Users resident in another EU member state can also lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority of their habitual residence or place of work.
