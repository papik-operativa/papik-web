BLOCK 6 · FAQ · 7 QUESTIONS

``` Frequently asked questions

```

``` Q · Can I personalise the house I buy from PAPIK as developer?

A · It depends on the stage of construction. If the house is finished, only final touches (furniture, fittings). If it is under construction, we can adjust specification details. If it is still at project stage, there is more room, always within what the contract allows.

──────────────

Q · What exactly does "turnkey" include?

A · The finished house with equipped kitchen, complete bathrooms, flooring and paint finishes, active utility connections, energy performance certificate, and certificate of occupancy. It does not include loose furniture, white goods, or decoration.

──────────────

Q · Is green financing or a green mortgage available?

A · Yes. Several Spanish banks offer mortgages with preferential terms for high-efficiency homes, often called green mortgages. We can present the technical file directly to the lender to support approval. For non-resident buyers, a separate set of products and loan-to-value criteria apply, and we can introduce you to brokers familiar with cross-border applications.

──────────────

Q · How long does it take to deliver a PAPIK development?

A · It depends on the stage. A finished house has immediate delivery after signing. A house under construction has a fixed date written into the contract. A house at project stage has an indicative timeline at the moment of reservation, confirmed in the final contract.

──────────────

Q · How does the contract protect me if there are delays?

A · The contract includes financial penalties per day of delay, full refund of the reservation if the works miss the schedule beyond a defined window, and bank guarantees on any sums paid during construction.

──────────────

Q · What sets a PAPIK development apart from other in-house developers?

A · We build to the same quality level for our developments as for a private commission. Proprietary Eskimohaus® construction system (CLT-based prefabrication), Passivhaus certifications, and a 30-year track record. The difference is not the price, it is the rigour.

──────────────

Q · Do you work with non-resident buyers?

A · Yes. Working with non-resident buyers, expats, and international families relocating to Catalonia is a core part of our practice. We coordinate the full purchase with specialised tax advisors, immigration lawyers, and notaries, so the fiscal, legal, and residency dimensions are aligned with the property purchase from day one. NIE numbers, opening of a Spanish bank account, golden visa pathways where applicable, and ongoing tax representation are handled through our trusted network. ```

CRITICAL NOTE TO THE COPYWRITER · DISAMBIGUATION

``` The page must make clear WITHIN THE FIRST 3 SECONDS OF SCROLL that here you BUY a product, you do not COMMISSION construction.

Any ambiguity generates misaligned leads: a private owner with a plot who thinks this is the place to build (should go to /en/construction); a private buyer without a plot who wants to buy (here, perfect); a wealth investor (should go to /en/wealth). For the international audience, add a third filter: relocating families and non-resident buyers belong here, even if their case involves a separate plot search later.

The HERO BLOCK + the subhead are the first filter. BLOCK 7 cross-linking is the second filter. If we hesitate between two words ("offer" vs "sell"), the more unambiguous one always wins for the audience type. ```
