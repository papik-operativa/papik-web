Valldoreix Extension

Valldoreix, Sant Cugat del Vallès (Vallès Occidental). Retrofit and extension of a 1935 dwelling with timber structure and high-performance envelope.

90 m²2012Retrofit

00Description

The extension of a 1935 single-family home in Valldoreix posed a twofold challenge: respect the character of the original volume while incorporating the constructive criteria that today define passive architecture. The solution relied on a prefabricated timber structure sourced from sustainably managed forests, executed with a ventilated facade and continuous vapour barrier.

The insulated envelope reached a U-value of 0.16 W/m²K, well below the 0.53 W/m²K then required by the Spanish Technical Building Code. Workshop prefabrication enabled a clean execution and significantly shorter site times, an advantage that mattered especially because the dwelling remained occupied during the works.

The project was selected for the 2012 Endesa Awards in the retrofit category, as an example of how Passivhaus criteria can be applied to existing buildings without compromising architectural coherence.
