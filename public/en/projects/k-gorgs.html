K-Gorgs

Barberà del Vallès2021132 m2

00Description

Passive house in Barberà del Vallès designed to Passivhaus criteria to reach near-zero energy demand (nZEB), with light timber frame and an exterior SATE finish.

The design prioritises a continuous thermal envelope and high air-tightness, verified with a blower-door test. Passivhaus-grade joinery and mechanical ventilation with heat recovery deliver stable thermal comfort and low energy use.

Dry construction with light timber frame allows tight execution timelines and rigorous control over construction details. The SATE finish adds exterior inertia and eliminates thermal bridges where slabs meet the façade.
