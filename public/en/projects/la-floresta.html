Project

Passive House in La Floresta

La Floresta, Sant Cugat del Vallès

2017120 m²Location: La Floresta

00Description

Single-family home in the village of La Floresta, an EMD of Sant Cugat del Vallès in the Vallès Occidental region. It was PAPIK's first project to combine a CLT (cross-laminated timber) structure with blown cellulose insulation, a combination that absorbs more CO₂ during construction than it emits.

The CLT structure builds walls, slabs and roof from large cross-laminated timber panels, factory-made and assembled on site in a short schedule. Blown cellulose fills the insulation cavity continuously and without thermal bridges, using a recycled-origin material.

The external finish is a ventilated larch-wood facade that ages naturally and blends with the wooded setting of La Floresta. The home runs on mechanical ventilation with heat recovery and a high-efficiency envelope that keeps operating demand very low.
