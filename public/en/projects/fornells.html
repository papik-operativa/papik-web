00Description

Detached single-family home in Fornells de la Selva, built with light timber framing and an EIFS exterior cladding for high energy efficiency.

The project relies on a continuous envelope with generous insulation thicknesses, high-performance windows, and heat-recovery ventilation. The interior layout is organised on ground and first floor, with openings calculated to capture southern sun and protect the east and west exposures.

Compared with conventional construction, the estimated energy savings are substantial thanks to the combination of insulation, airtightness, and efficient systems. The Eskimohaus system adapts well to the climate of the Selva plain, with humid winters and hot summers.
