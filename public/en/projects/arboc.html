00Description

Detached single-family home in l'Arboç, Baix Penedès, with two joined volumes built in light timber framing and a combined EIFS and ventilated timber envelope.

The project was conceived under the Passivhaus standard with a focus on low energy consumption. PAPIK Group worked with a passive-design studio to refine orientation, thermal bridges, and roof geometry to maximise winter solar gain and protect from summer heat.

The build confirms the viability of Passivhaus in the Penedès region and consolidates one of the early certifications in the area. Energy savings versus a conventional construction are substantial thanks to continuous insulation, heat-recovery ventilation, and high-quality openings.
