K-Denbas

Sant Privat d'en Bas, La Garrotxa · Detached single-family

Eskimohaus2023198 m²

00Description

Detached single-family home of 198 m² in the municipality of Sant Privat d'en Bas, within La Garrotxa region, built with the Eskimohaus light timber frame system. The plot opens onto a rural setting of oaks and meadows, with views over the valley and nearby streams.

The programme distributes three bedrooms, two bathrooms, a study, a kitchen-dining room, two terraces and a multipurpose room. The plan is organised around solar orientation and views to maximise natural light capture and articulate the living spaces with the outdoor areas.

The Eskimohaus system applies a continuous envelope, thermal bridge control and high airtightness verified by blower-door test. Dry construction with timber from managed forests fits the environmental sensitivity of the area and shortens construction time compared to conventional wet systems.
