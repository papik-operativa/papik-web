BLOCK 2 · HOW WE WORK · 4 PHASES

``` How we work

Four phases, no more and no less.

```

``` 01 · INITIAL CONVERSATION

Private meeting under NDA. We get to know each other. There are no proposals or numbers yet. The conversation is bilateral: you assess us, we assess you.

──────────────

02 · MUTUAL ASSESSMENT

You assess our track record, team and methodological proposals. We assess your profile, capital, timeline and alignment of values. If the assessment is positive for both parties, we move forward.

──────────────

03 · STRUCTURING THE VEHICLE

Design of an ad hoc company or private vehicle for the specific project. Legal, fiscal and operational framework defined by independent counsel (you and we hold separate advice).

──────────────

04 · DEVELOPMENT AND OPERATION

We build the project. You participate in the governance of the vehicle in accordance with what has been agreed. Exit or operation in line with the mandate (asset sale, refinancing or long-term operation). ```

`` PAPIK assesses before proposing. No project reaches phase 03 without both parties having validated phase 02. ``

BLOCK 7 · FAQ · 7 QUESTIONS

``` Frequently asked questions

```

``` Q · What is the minimum ticket?

A · It depends on the project and the structured vehicle. We define this in the initial conversation. We do not publish ranges because each case has its own scale.

──────────────

Q · How is the joint investment vehicle structured?

A · Case by case. It can be an ad hoc company, a trust or other legal forms, always with independent legal advice for both parties.

──────────────

Q · Who decides the investments of the vehicle?

A · Governance is defined in the vehicle's shareholder agreement. It is usually co-management, with key decisions taken collegially between the parties.

──────────────

Q · What is the typical exit?

A · Asset sale, refinancing or long-term operation, in accordance with the mandate agreed when the vehicle is constituted. There is no "standard exit": it depends on the specific project.

──────────────

Q · Does PAPIK contribute its own capital to the vehicle?

A · In some cases yes, in others only construction and management. This is defined case by case during the structuring phase.

──────────────

Q · How long does it take from the first meeting to a concrete proposal?

A · Between four and twelve weeks, depending on the complexity of the mutual assessment and the state of the specific project.

──────────────

Q · Do you work with non-resident investors?

A · Yes, with specific fiscal and legal structuring adapted to the partner's country of residence. Coordination with specialised advisors.

──────────────

Q · Is there confidentiality?

A · Total. An NDA is signed before the first substantive meeting. No information about partners or projects in portfolio is shared without explicit consent. ```
