Technical articles and case studies

Thirty years building Passivhaus in Catalonia have given us material to write about. We publish what we learn on site: technologies, planning decisions, financing and territorial knowledge.

We publish articles when we have something concrete to say. We do not write generic opinion pieces about the sector. Each article comes from a real case, a specific commission or a question we have been asked more than three times in the office.

The articles are organised in four areas, depending on the kind of question they answer. Passivhaus construction takes the largest share, because it is our main activity. The rest covers economic decisions, special cases and corporate communication.
