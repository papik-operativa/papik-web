PROJECTS

Homes built by PAPIK Group

30 years, over 100 builds in Catalonia, the Balearics and Andorra.

The PAPIK Group portfolio gathers three decades of residential work across the territory, with a heavy concentration in Sant Cugat del Vallès, Bellaterra, Valldoreix, Matadepera and the rest of the Vallès Occidental county, and a consolidated presence in the Maresme, Anoia, Garraf, Baix Llobregat, Costa Brava, the Balearics and Andorra. Most projects are detached single-family homes built from scratch; the portfolio also includes deep retrofits, extensions, rooftop additions and a four-unit Passivhaus Plus development for sale.

We build in lightweight timber frame using our proprietary Eskimohaus® system, and in CLT (cross-laminated timber) where the brief calls for it. Seven of the recent builds have reached Passivhaus certification in one of its variants. K-del-Carril (Sant Cugat, 2018) was the first certified Passivhaus in the municipality; PassivPalau, the second Passivhaus in Catalonia. K-Iturbi (Bellaterra) and K-Matadepera (CLT) anchor the certified line at Tier 1; K-Vall d'Or takes the development arm to Passivhaus Plus.

Each project page concentrates the essential technical detail: typology, year, construction system, performance level, location at municipality and county scale. We do not publish exact addresses or owner-identifying data.
