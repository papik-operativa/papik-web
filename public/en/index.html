In-house system

Eskimohaus® · our construction system

After thirty years of building, we developed a system that combines the principles of the German Passivhaus standard with Mediterranean materials and climate.

01 · THERMAL ENVELOPE

Insulate before generating

Insulation and airtightness as the foundation of the system. We reduce energy losses before thinking about generation. An efficient home starts at the wall.

02 · VENTILATION WITH HEAT RECOVERY

Fresh air, zero loss

Constant fresh air without energy loss. Zehnder heat-recovery systems keep the home healthy and efficient regardless of outside conditions.

03 · EFFICIENT STRUCTURE

Materials that age well

Cross-laminated timber (CLT), light timber framing or mixed systems depending on the project. Natural, renewable materials with minimal impact on the site.

An Eskimohaus® home uses up to 90% less energy than a conventional one.

About us

Thirty years building the future of the home

In 1994, Papik Fisas Moreno founded PAPIK in Sant Cugat del Vallès with a different vision: homes that last, not homes that sell. Construction as craft, natural materials, energy efficiency before it had a name.

In 2010 we built the second certified Passivhaus home in Catalonia. In 2014 we developed our own construction system, Eskimohaus®. Today, more than 100 homes built and 36 documented online give every new project a solid base of experience.

We continue working in Catalonia, the Balearic Islands and Andorra with the same principle: a well-built home is the most profitable long-term investment — for those who live in it and for those who come after.
