BLOCK 3 · PLANNING PARTICULARS

`` What you should know before building in Sant Quirze ``

Current local urban plan (POUM)

`` The local urban plan (POUM) of Sant Quirze del Vallès was approved in 2003 and has had subsequent specific amendments. The consolidated version is available through the Catalan Urban Planning Registry (RPUC) of the Generalitat and through the municipal planning portal. ``

Predominant land typologies

`` The detached or semi-detached single-family typology predominates in Les Fonts, Mas Duran and Sant Quirze Parc. The party-wall multi-family typology is concentrated in the historic centre. Terraced houses appear in sectors with derived planning. Non-developable land has partial protection to the south, in zones bordering Collserola. ``

Common planning parameters (detached single-family residential)

`` The minimum plot size in detached single-family residential is typically between 300 and 700 m² depending on the area. Net buildable capacity ranges between 0.4 and 0.6 m²st/m²s. Maximum occupancy is between 30% and 40%. Standard height is ground floor plus one under cornice, plus roof. Standard setbacks are 3 to 5 m from the road and 3 m or H/2 from the boundary. ``

Landscape restrictions

`` Sectors bordering Collserola have specific landscape restrictions. Consolidated trees in the historic core are protected by municipal ordinance. ``

Permit process

`` The electronic platform is the Sant Quirze e-government portal integrated with the Catalan e-Government platform (AOC). Indicative resolution times range between 4 and 12 weeks. The ICIO tax applies according to the current Tax Ordinance. ``

⚠ Verify specific data against the planning data sheet of the plot.

BLOCK 5 · NOTABLE AREAS OF SANT QUIRZE

``` Residential areas of the municipality

THE HISTORIC CENTRE concentrates party-wall multi-family and consolidated single-family housing, with the option of retrofit and extension on existing homes. LES FONTS is mid residential with single-family plots and good connections to Terrassa and Sabadell. MAS DURAN is a recent residential sector with single-family and terraced typologies, with active derived planning. SANT QUIRZE PARC is a mixed industrial-residential area with occasional residential sectors and wide plots. ```
