```
[H2]
Why building in Argentona now

Argentona is a town of around twelve thousand inhabitants with
a solid residential track record. Its proximity to Mataró and
the direct exit to the C-32 motorway place it within a short
hour of any point in the Barcelona metropolitan area, without
losing its village character.

The residential market is built around detached and
semi-detached single-family homes in areas such as Madà, Sant
Miquel del Cros and Can Mainou, with plots that allow complete
functional briefs without the planning pressures of the front
coastline. The connection with the natural setting of the
coastal Maresme is one of the factors that weighs most for
families who choose the town.

PAPIK has a direct case in Argentona and several nearby
projects. You speak with a team that knows the ground.

## BLOCK 3 · PLANNING PARTICULARS

```
[H2]
What you should know before building in Argentona
```

### Current local urban plan (POUM)

```
The local urban plan (POUM) of Argentona was approved in 2008
and has been updated with subsequent specific amendments. The
consolidated version is available through the Catalan Urban
Planning Registry (RPUC) of the Generalitat and through the
municipal planning portal.
```

### Predominant land typologies

```
The detached single-family typology predominates in Madà, Sant
Miquel del Cros and Can Mainou, with medium and large plots.
The party-wall multi-family typology is concentrated in the
historic centre, with a consolidated narrow-street fabric.
Non-developable land sectors with landscape protection appear
around the urban core, especially on the slopes facing the
coastal corridor.
```

### Common planning parameters (detached single-family residential)

```
The minimum plot size in detached single-family residential is
typically between 400 and 800 m² depending on the sub-zone.
Net buildable capacity ranges between 0.3 and 0.5 m²st/m²s.
Maximum occupancy is between 25% and 35%. Standard height is
ground floor plus one under cornice, plus roof. Standard
setbacks are 4 to 5 m from the road and 3 m or H/2 from the
boundary.
```

### Landscape restrictions

```
Integration with the natural setting of the coastal Maresme
is one of the axes of the POUM. Sectors bordering non-developable
land have specific volumetric and finishing restrictions.
Consolidated native vegetation is protected by municipal
ordinance in certain areas.
```

### Permit process

```
The electronic platform is the Argentona e-government portal
integrated with the Catalan e-Government platform (AOC).
Indicative resolution times range between 6 and 14 weeks, with
variability depending on the workload of the planning office
and any sectoral consultations. The ICIO tax applies according
to the current Tax Ordinance.
```

⚠ Verify specific data against the planning data sheet of the plot.

```
[H2]
Residential areas of the municipality

[Editorial text]

THE HISTORIC CENTRE concentrates the party-wall multi-family
fabric and consolidated single-family homes with options for
deep retrofit and, in some cases, extension. MADÀ is a
single-family residential sector with medium and large plots,
well oriented and with gentle topography. SANT MIQUEL DEL CROS
is a distinct residential nucleus, with detached and
semi-detached typologies, well connected by road. CAN MAINOU
keeps a quiet residential character with wide plots and a low
density that favours projects with a generous brief.

```
Q · Do you usually work in Argentona?

A · Yes. We have a direct case in the municipality (K-Argentona)

──────────────

Q · What minimum plot size does the City Council require in Argentona?

A · It depends on the area. In detached single-family it is
typically between 400 and 800 m². The specific planning data
sheet of the plot defines the exact applicable parameter.

──────────────

Q · How long does a building permit take in Argentona?

A · Resolution usually takes between 6 and 14 weeks from the
complete submission of the documentation. The deadline can be
extended if heritage or roads sectoral consultations are
involved.

──────────────

Q · Can Passivhaus be built in areas with landscape restrictions?

A · Yes. The Passivhaus construction system is independent from
the exterior formal expression. We adapt volumes, materials and
finishes to the requirements of the POUM and the municipal
ordinance, while keeping certifiable energy performance.

──────────────

Q · What indicative budget should be planned for in Argentona?

A · The construction cost with PAPIK is the same across the
Maresme; it varies depending on the system, brief and finishes.
What sets one project apart from another is the plot. Use the
budget configurator or request a visit to firm up figures.
```
