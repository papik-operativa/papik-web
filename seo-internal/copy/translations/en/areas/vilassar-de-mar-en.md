## BLOCK 1 · WHY VILASSAR DE MAR

```
[H2]
Why building in Vilassar de Mar now

Vilassar de Mar is a town of about twenty-one thousand
inhabitants with a consolidated mid-premium residential profile.
Its strength comes from three combined factors: an accessible
seafront, a RENFE R1 commuter station in the centre with
continuous frequency to Barcelona, and a commercial and service
fabric scaled for a town with its own identity.

The residential market is concentrated in detached and
semi-detached single-family homes in the upper part of the
municipality, with plots that allow a complete family brief.
Areas such as the Vimar sector or the Camí del Mig axis combine
favourable orientation, gentle topography and good inland
connection with the N-II and C-32 networks. For families
seeking a balance between coastal life and metropolitan
mobility, Vilassar is one of the most stable options in the
Maresme.
```

## BLOCK 3 · PLANNING PARTICULARS

```
[H2]
What you should know before building in Vilassar de Mar
```

### Current local urban plan (POUM)

```
The local urban plan (POUM) of Vilassar de Mar was approved in
2003 and has been subject to subsequent specific amendments.
The consolidated version is available through the Catalan Urban
Planning Registry (RPUC) of the Generalitat and through the
municipal planning portal.
```

### Predominant land typologies

```
The detached and semi-detached single-family typology
predominates in the upper part of the municipality and in
sectors such as Vimar, with medium-sized plots. The party-wall
multi-family typology is concentrated in the historic centre
and along the axis parallel to the coastline, with consolidated
frontages. Terraced residential sectors appear in more recent
derived plans. Non-developable land remains protected on the
northern slopes, in transition areas towards Cabrils and
Vilassar de Dalt.
```

### Common planning parameters (detached single-family residential)

```
The minimum plot size in detached single-family residential is
typically between 300 and 600 m² depending on the sub-zone.
Net buildable capacity ranges between 0.4 and 0.6 m²st/m²s.
Maximum occupancy is between 30% and 40%. Standard height is
ground floor plus one under cornice, plus roof. Standard
setbacks are 3 to 5 m from the road and 3 m or H/2 from the
boundary.
```

### Landscape restrictions

```
Proximity to the seafront generates specific constraints on
orientation, heights and finishes for facades visible from the
promenade. Sectors bordering the RENFE railway line have
railway easements. Consolidated pine vegetation in the north of
the municipality is protected by municipal ordinance in certain
areas.
```

### Permit process

```
The electronic platform is the Vilassar de Mar e-government
portal integrated with the Catalan e-Government platform (AOC).
Indicative resolution times range between 6 and 12 weeks, with
variability depending on the workload of the planning office
and any sectoral consultations from the coastal authority or
heritage. The ICIO tax applies according to the current Tax
Ordinance.
```

⚠ Verify specific data against the planning data sheet of the plot.

```
Q · What minimum plot size does the City Council require in Vilassar de Mar?

A · It depends on the area. In detached single-family it is
typically between 300 and 600 m². The specific planning data
sheet of the plot defines the exact applicable parameter.

──────────────

Q · How long does a building permit take in Vilassar de Mar?

A · Resolution usually takes between 6 and 12 weeks from the
complete submission of the documentation. The deadline can be
extended if coastal or heritage sectoral consultations are
involved.

──────────────

Q · Can Passivhaus be built close to the seafront?

A · Yes. The Passivhaus envelope is particularly effective in
coastal climates, where airtightness and ventilation with heat
recovery control humidity and salinity. We adapt materials and
finishes to marine exposure without losing certifiable energy
performance.

──────────────

Q · Is Vilassar more expensive than neighbouring Maresme towns?

A · The construction cost with PAPIK is the same across the
Maresme. The difference between municipalities is the price of
the land. Vilassar de Mar has a plot price typically higher
than Argentona or Premià de Dalt, and lower than Cabrils or
Llavaneres in broad terms. Each operation is assessed
individually.
```
