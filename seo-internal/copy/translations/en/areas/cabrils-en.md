## BLOCK 1 · WHY CABRILS

```
[H2]
Why build in Cabrils now

Cabrils is a municipality of around seven thousand residents on
the inland slope of the Maresme. Its gentle hill topography, low
residential density and proximity to both Mataró and the
Barcelona corridor make it one of the most sought-after areas
for premium single-family housing in the county.

The residential market is dominated by plots of typically 800 to
2,000 m², with low-density detached typologies. The proximity to
Cabrera de Mar and Vilassar de Mar combines a village character
with quick access to services and Renfe (Spanish national rail)
stations. Families who choose Cabrils value plot orientation,
open views and acoustic calm.

PAPIK does not yet have a direct case in Cabrils, but our
operational knowledge of the Maresme is solid. Would you like
to be the first project?
```

## BLOCK 3 · PLANNING PARTICULARS

```
[H2]
What you should know before building in Cabrils
```

### Current local urban plan (POUM)

```
The current local urban plan in Cabrils has subsequent specific
amendments to the original approval, and the consolidated version
is available through the Catalan Urban Planning Registry (RPUC)
of the Generalitat (Catalan government) and the municipal
planning portal. The specific planning data sheet for each plot
is the reference document.
```

### Predominant land typologies

```
The detached single-family typology predominates throughout the
municipality, with mid-size and large plots. Density is low and
residential areas are integrated with the natural hill
environment. Non-developable land has a significant presence on
the perimeter, with specific landscape protection that
constrains adjoining sectors.
```

### Common planning parameters (detached single-family residential)

```
The minimum plot size in detached single-family residential is
typically between 600 and 1,200 m² depending on the sub-zone,
with sectors reaching 2,000 m². Net buildable capacity ranges
between 0.2 and 0.4 m²st/m²s. Maximum occupancy is between 20%
and 30%. Standard height is ground floor plus one under cornice,
plus roof. Standard setbacks are 4 to 5 m from the road and 3 m
or H/2 from the boundary.
```

### Landscape restrictions

```
Integration with the natural environment is a core axis of the
plan in Cabrils. Sectors bordering non-developable land have
specific restrictions on volume and finishes. Consolidated native
vegetation is protected by municipal ordinance in defined zones,
particularly the Mediterranean tree mass typical of the inland
Maresme.
```

### Permit process

```
The electronic platform is the Cabrils e-government portal
integrated with the Catalan e-Government platform (AOC).
Indicative resolution times range between 6 and 14 weeks, with
possible extension if heritage or landscape sectoral consultations
intervene. The ICIO tax applies according to the current Tax
Ordinance.
```

⚠ Verify specific data against the planning data sheet of the plot.

```

──────────────

Q · What minimum plot size does the City Council require in Cabrils?

A · It depends on the sub-zone. In detached single-family it is
typically between 600 and 1,200 m², with sectors reaching
2,000 m². The specific planning data sheet defines the exact
parameter applicable to each plot.

──────────────

Q · How long does a building permit take in Cabrils?

A · Resolution usually takes between 6 and 14 weeks from complete
submission of the documentation. There may be an extension if
heritage or landscape sectoral consultations intervene, which is
fairly common on plots bordering non-developable land.

──────────────

Q · Can you build Passivhaus on sloped plots?

A · Yes. Most plots in Cabrils have a slope. The Passivhaus
construction system is independent of plot topography. We adapt
the layout, slabs and envelope to the terrain, while keeping
certifiable energy performance.

──────────────

Q · What indicative budget should I expect in Cabrils?

A · The construction cost with PAPIK is the same throughout the
Maresme; it varies by system, programme and finishes. The main
difference between municipalities is land value. Use the budget
configurator or request a visit to refine the figure.
```
