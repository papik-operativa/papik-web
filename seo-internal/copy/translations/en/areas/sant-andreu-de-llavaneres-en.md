## BLOCK 3 · PLANNING PARTICULARS

```
[H2]
What you should know before building in Sant Andreu de Llavaneres
```

### Current local urban plan (POUM)

```
The local urban plan (POUM) of Sant Andreu de Llavaneres was
approved in 2007 and has had subsequent specific amendments.
The consolidated version is available through the Catalan
Urban Planning Registry (RPUC) of the Generalitat and through
the municipal planning portal.
```

### Predominant land typologies

```
The detached single-family typology predominates in
developments and consolidated sectors such as La Crestamar,
with often wide plots and sloped topography. The party-wall
multi-family typology is concentrated in the historic centre,
with a narrow-street fabric and a village character.
Non-developable land sectors with high protection appear on
the upper slopes facing the sea and around the rural core.
```

### Common planning parameters (detached single-family residential)

```
The minimum plot size in detached single-family residential is
typically between 600 and 1,200 m² depending on the sub-zone,
with significantly larger sizes in some sectors. Net buildable
capacity ranges between 0.25 and 0.45 m²st/m²s. Maximum
occupancy is between 20% and 30%. Standard height is ground
floor plus one under cornice, plus roof. Standard setbacks are
5 m from the road and 3 m or H/2 from the boundary.
```

### Landscape restrictions

```
Landscape integration is central to the POUM. Sectors with
dominant views over the coastal corridor have specific
volumetric and finishing restrictions to preserve the profile
of the municipality as seen from the sea and from the C-32
motorway. Consolidated trees and native vegetation are
protected by municipal ordinance in certain areas.
```

### Permit process

```
The electronic platform is the Sant Andreu de Llavaneres
e-government portal integrated with the Catalan e-Government
platform (AOC). Indicative resolution times range between 6
and 14 weeks, with possible extension if heritage or coastal
sectoral consultations are involved. The ICIO tax applies
according to the current Tax Ordinance.
```

⚠ Verify specific data against the planning data sheet of the plot.

```
[H2]
Residential areas of the municipality

[Editorial text]

THE HISTORIC CENTRE concentrates the party-wall multi-family
fabric and consolidated single-family homes, with a village
character and options for deep retrofit. CONSOLIDATED
RESIDENTIAL DEVELOPMENTS, distributed across the slopes of the
municipality, offer single-family plots of medium and large
size, with often sloped topography and privileged orientations.
LA CRESTAMAR is one of the most recognised residential sectors,
with wide plots and good sea views. PERI-URBAN AREAS at the

with very low densities and demanding landscape standards.
```
