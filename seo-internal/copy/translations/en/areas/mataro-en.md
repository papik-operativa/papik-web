## BLOCK 1 · WHY MATARÓ

```
[H2]
Why building in Mataró now

Mataró is the capital of the Maresme: around one hundred and
thirty thousand inhabitants, a reference hospital, a RENFE R1
commuter hub with high frequency to Barcelona, direct exits to
the C-32 motorway and the N-II coastal road, and an active
university and technology network around the TecnoCampus. This
status of county capital explains a residential market more
varied than in neighbouring towns: there is urban party-wall
product, mid-quality multi-family and, in specific areas,
premium single-family clusters.

The residential market is built around areas such as Cirera,
Vista Alegre and La Llantia, which combine single-family plots
with favourable topography and southeast orientation towards
the sea. The centre and the seafront concentrate denser
multi-family fabric, with retrofit interventions and, in some
cases, opportunities for "demolish and rebuild" operations.
Price levels are more moderate than in Llavaneres or Cabrils
and offer favourable value for money for a family brief.

PAPIK does not yet have a direct case in Mataró. You can be
the first.
```

## BLOCK 3 · PLANNING PARTICULARS

```
[H2]
What you should know before building in Mataró
```

### Current local urban plan (POUM)

```
The General Urban Plan of Mataró was initially approved in 1996
and has had numerous specific and sectoral subsequent
amendments. A revision is currently in progress. The
consolidated version is available through the Catalan Urban
Planning Registry (RPUC) of the Generalitat and through the
municipal planning portal. Given the scale of the city, it is
advisable to verify the specific planning data sheet of the
plot before any feasibility study.
```

### Predominant land typologies

```
The party-wall multi-family typology predominates in the
historic centre and along the axis parallel to the coast, with
consolidated fabric and medium-sized streets. The detached or
semi-detached single-family typology is concentrated in the
upper part of the municipality: Cirera, Vista Alegre, La
Llantia and sectors bordering Argentona. Terraced residential
areas appear in more recent derived plans. Non-developable
land remains protected in the northern sector, in transition
areas towards the Coastal Range.
```

### Common planning parameters (detached single-family residential)

```
The minimum plot size in detached single-family residential is
typically between 300 and 800 m² depending on the sub-zone,
with more generous sizes in the upper part. Net buildable
capacity ranges between 0.4 and 0.7 m²st/m²s. Maximum occupancy
is between 30% and 40%. Standard height is ground floor plus
one under cornice, plus roof. Standard setbacks are 3 to 5 m
from the road and 3 m or H/2 from the boundary. The diversity
of typologies in the city makes it especially advisable to
consult the specific data sheet.
```

### Landscape restrictions

```
The historic centre has a Special Heritage Protection Plan with
regulations on facades, height and use. Proximity to the
seafront generates constraints in sectors bordering the
promenade. Specific areas of the Coastal Range have specific
landscape protection. Consolidated vegetation in several
residential sectors is protected by municipal ordinance.
```

### Permit process

```
The electronic platform is the Mataró e-government portal
integrated with the Catalan e-Government platform (AOC).
Indicative resolution times range between 8 and 16 weeks, with
variability depending on the workload of the planning office
and any sectoral consultations. As Mataró is the county
capital, the volume of permit traffic is higher than in
neighbouring municipalities. The ICIO tax applies according to
the current Tax Ordinance.
```

⚠ Verify specific data against the planning data sheet of the plot.

```
Residential areas of the municipality

[Editorial text]

THE HISTORIC CENTRE concentrates the densest party-wall
multi-family fabric, with consolidated frontages and a specific
heritage protection ordinance; it is the typical area for deep
retrofit. CIRERA is a consolidated single-family residential
sector in the upper part, with medium-sized plots, good
southeast orientation and favourable topography, considered a
premium cluster of the municipality. VISTA ALEGRE keeps a quiet
residential character with wide plots and a generous family
brief, in a sector bordering Argentona. LA LLANTIA is a mixed
residential sector with detached and semi-detached typologies,
well oriented. THE COASTAL EIXAMPLE is the multi-family fabric
parallel to the promenade, with dense residential product and
specific retrofit or rebuild interventions. ROCAFONDA and
CERDANYOLA are mixed residential sectors with active derived
plans.
```

## BLOCK 5 · LOCAL FAQ · 5 QUESTIONS

```
Q · Is Mataró a premium or a moderate residential market?

A · Both. The diversity of the city is precisely its appeal:
most of the market is moderate, with party-wall multi-family
and urban single-family homes, while specific areas like
Cirera, Vista Alegre or La Llantia work as premium clusters
with single-family plots and favourable orientation. The
construction cost with PAPIK is the same; the difference
between operations is the plot.

──────────────

Q · What minimum plot size does the City Council require in Mataró?

A · It depends very much on the sub-zone. In detached
single-family it is typically between 300 and 800 m². Given
the typological diversity of the city, it is essential to
consult the specific planning data sheet of the plot to
determine the exact applicable parameter.

──────────────

Q · How long does a building permit take in Mataró?

A · Resolution usually takes between 8 and 16 weeks from the
complete submission of the documentation. The permit traffic
of the county capital can generate variability compared with
smaller municipalities.

──────────────

Q · Can "demolish and rebuild" be done in the centre of Mataró?

A · Yes, on plots with existing buildings outside the heritage
protection areas. The historic centre has a Special Protection
Plan that limits certain types of intervention. In the rest of
the municipality, the operation is technically viable with a
prior structural study, the current ordinance and lawfulness
conditions. PAPIK has experience in this type of operation
across the Maresme.

──────────────

Q · Do you usually work in Mataró?

A · We do not yet have a direct project in Mataró, but we work
regularly across the Maresme. Our closest cases are just 5 km
away: K-Llavaneres and K-Argentona. Site logistics are
consolidated in the county and can cover Mataró without
friction. You could be the first PAPIK Passivhaus project in
the county capital.
```
