## BLOCK 3 · WHAT WE COVER · 4 SERVICE CATEGORIES

```
[H2]
What a PAPIK retrofit covers

[Subhead · 1 line]
Four areas of intervention, combinable case by case.

[Grid 2x2 desktop · 1x4 mobile · no iconography]
```

### 3.1 THERMAL ENVELOPE

```
[H3]
Thermal envelope

The thermal envelope covers the insulation of the exterior façade
with SATE (external thermal insulation system) or interior insulation
where SATE is not viable, the insulation of the roof and upper ceiling,
the replacement of windows with high-performance wood, PVC or mixed
systems, and the elimination of structural thermal bridges.

It is the intervention with the greatest impact on energy savings.
Acting on the envelope alone can reduce consumption by 30% to 50%.
```

### 3.2 MECHANICAL SYSTEMS

```
[H3]
Mechanical systems and climate control

Mechanical systems cover ventilation with heat recovery (Zehnder or
equivalent), aerothermal heat pumps as a replacement for gas boilers,
heat pumps with underfloor heating, and efficient cooling.

The transition from gas to aerothermal is subsidised by NGEU
programme 4. Heat-recovery ventilation is almost mandatory once the
envelope is well insulated, since without it the house cannot
breathe properly.
```

### 3.3 ENERGY GENERATION

```
[H3]
Renewable energy generation

On-site generation includes photovoltaic panels for self-consumption,
a self-consumption battery with partial autonomy, an electric vehicle
charger, and aerothermal systems for domestic hot water.

The combination of "envelope + aerothermal + photovoltaic" can bring
the dwelling close to nearly zero energy (nZEB) or even to a positive
energy balance.
```

### 3.4 CERTIFICATION AND VERIFICATION

```
[H3]
Certification and technical verification

The verification phase includes the energy certificate before and
after the works, the blower-door test to measure airtightness,
thermography to detect non-visible losses, and EnerPHit (Passivhaus
standard for retrofits) certification.

EnerPHit is the equivalent of Passivhaus for existing dwellings.
When the technical case allows it, we offer it as the highest
retrofit standard available.
```

## BLOCK 7 · FAQ · 8 QUESTIONS

```
[H2]
Frequently asked questions

[Accordion]
```

```
Q · Which Next Generation subsidy can I apply to my home?

A · It depends on the energy savings achieved by the retrofit, the
type of dwelling and, in some programmes, on income. NGEU subsidies
are intended for Spanish fiscal residents. You can estimate it with
our calculator; we confirm it during the free technical visit.

──────────────

Q · How long does the aid procedure take?

A · Subsidy approval usually takes 3 to 6 months from the date of
application. In the meantime, you can move forward with the project
if you choose to.

──────────────

Q · Is there a cap on the eligible budget?

A · Yes. Each NGEU programme has its own caps per dwelling and type
of intervention. We review them case by case to maximise the
applicable aid.

──────────────

Q · What if the subsidy is not approved?

A · The project remains viable with private financing. Many banks
offer a green mortgage with a bonus for efficient homes. We guide
you through the available options.

──────────────

Q · Do we have to leave the house during the works?

A · It depends on the intervention. An exterior façade (SATE) or
roof retrofit can be carried out with the house occupied. A full
retrofit may require moving out for a few months. We plan it with you.

──────────────

Q · What happens if hidden problems appear during the works?

A · Every retrofit allows for unforeseen issues. We handle them
transparently and propose solutions before continuing. The diagnosis
phase reduces these surprises but never eliminates them entirely.

──────────────

Q · Do you work on old flats in central Barcelona (19th century)?

A · Yes. Each case requires a specific assessment for structural
compatibility and heritage requirements where the building is
listed. Retrofitting old Barcelona flats is one of our regular
areas of work.

──────────────

Q · Can a retrofit reach Passivhaus standard?

A · Yes. The equivalent certification for retrofits is called
EnerPHit. We offer it whenever the technical case allows (it is
the highest retrofit standard available today).
```
