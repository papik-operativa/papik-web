## BLOC 1 · What we are

```
[Eyebrow]
WHO WE ARE

[H2]
Passivhaus constructor with a proprietary system
```

PAPIK Group is a Catalan constructor specialised in Passivhaus housing, with its head office in Sant Cugat del Vallès. The company started in 1994 as a joinery and, over three decades, has evolved into a building group with its own construction system, Eskimohaus®, and a portfolio of projects across Catalonia, the Balearic Islands and Andorra. The operational mission is to build homes that last a century and consume up to ninety per cent less than a conventional build, with no compromise on aesthetics, comfort or market revaluation.

## BLOC 2 · What we do

```
[Eyebrow]
WHAT WE DO

[H2]
Four complementary business units
```

PAPIK's activity is organised around four business units. [Residential Passivhaus construction](/en/construction) is the historical and largest unit: detached homes from zero to turnkey, built with the Eskimohaus® system. [Real estate development](/en/development) delivers Passivhaus homes for direct sale, often in partnership with landowners. [Energy retrofit](/en/retrofit) covers EnerPHit for existing housing, as well as condominium retrofits with integrated management of European subsidies. Finally, the [wealth](/en/wealth) unit offers co-investment and joint asset management to owners who wish to monetise land or buildings without executing the project themselves.

## BLOC 3 · Why we do it this way

```
[Eyebrow]
WHY

[H2]
Passivhaus convictions, sustainable materials, generational thinking
```

The commitment to Passivhaus is technical and ethical, not stylistic. A home with a continuous envelope, verified airtightness and ventilation with heat recovery consumes a fraction of what a conventional build does, holds an indoor temperature stable throughout the year and ages without degrading. Add to this the priority placed on low-impact materials (timber structure, blown-in cellulose, cork, paints free of volatile compounds) and a generational view of every project: a PAPIK home is designed to be lived in, extended or refurbished by the next generation without starting from scratch. The detailed technical rationale is set out in the [five Passivhaus principles](/en/article-passivhaus-principles) and in the [key technologies guide](/en/article-passivhaus-technologies).

## BLOC 5 · Recognised credentials

```
[Eyebrow]
CREDENTIALS

[H2]
Certifications, marks and associations
```

PAPIK Group builds to the protocols of the Passivhaus Institut in Darmstadt and accredits its technical staff with the official Certified Passive House Designer / Tradesperson training. Structural materials come from FSC and PEFC certified supply chains, a guarantee of sustainable forest management. The company is a member of the Plataforma de Edificación Passivhaus (PEP), the leading Spanish association in the field, and collaborates regularly with Green Building Council España, the Cerdà Institute and local universities (UPC, La Salle) on applied research projects. Every build undergoes quality control with a blower-door test and, where appropriate, formal Passivhaus or EnerPHit certification.
