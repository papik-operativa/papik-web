## BLOCK 4 · ESKIMOHAUS® SYSTEM

```
[H2]
Eskimohaus®, our proprietary construction system

[Intro paragraph]
After thirty years of building, we have developed a system that combines the
principles of the German Passivhaus standard with Mediterranean materials and
climate. This is not about applying an imported technology, but about
integrating an international methodology with the specific conditions of the
territory.

[Subhead]
Four principles, nothing superfluous

[Grid 4 columns · no iconography, only typography and white space]
```

### Column 1 · THERMAL ENVELOPE

```
Continuous insulation, elimination of thermal bridges. We reduce energy
losses before thinking about generating energy, because an efficient home
starts at the wall.
```

### Column 2 · CONTROLLED AIRTIGHTNESS

```
Verified with a post-construction blower-door test. An airtight home is
not a home that does not breathe; it is a home that controls how it
breathes.
```

### Column 3 · VENTILATION WITH HEAT RECOVERY

```
Constant fresh air without energy loss. Zehnder heat recovery systems
keep the home healthy throughout the year.
```

### Column 4 · EFFICIENT STRUCTURE

```
Cross-laminated timber (CLT), light-frame timber or hybrid systems
depending on the project. Natural, renewable materials with minimal
impact on the plot.
```

## Changes from v1

This is the first English version, derived from CA v2. All long dashes have been removed from the source and that rule is preserved here. The microtrust strip uses commas for better cadency. The company history elevates the register and incorporates more substantive arguments (for example, the line "a well-built home is the most profitable long-term investment" instead of the earlier, more telegraphic phrasing). The paragraphs in Block 4 (Eskimohaus®) causally connect each principle with its technical effect. The cross-cutting "no iconography" rule is preserved.

Cultural adaptations specific to EN: formal "vostè" rendered as standard professional "you"; toponyms kept in Catalan with a one-time geographic gloss for international readers (Sant Cugat del Vallès, Maresme, Anoia); "Next Generation" rendered as "Next Generation EU recovery funds" on first mention; "clau en mà" rendered as "turnkey"; "particulars" implied through the broader "your home" framing; "promoció" rendered as "development" (the standard real estate term); "patrimonis" rendered as "wealth", reflecting the family-office and cross-border-investor audience; URLs remapped to the `/en/` prefix per the URL map.
